The Best Animated Films

My top 3 films of all-time.

Howls moving castle

You can never go wrong with a Ghibli film, all of their works are a 9/10 at the least! Gorgeous animation and story.

Your Name

A master piece from Makoto Shinkai, with beautiful artwork and story telling. A play on timeline making it a very fun watch!

A silent voice

I loved the original manga the film is based off of and the film delivered. Great character development with a satisfying ending.
